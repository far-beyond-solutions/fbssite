Our Services

FBS is Empowering Bronx Businesses and Advancing a More Equitable, Democratic, and Sustainable Economy. FBS is an online marketplace that connects Bronx institutions and nonprofit organizations with high-road, local businesses in order to meet their local purchasing goals and support a more sustainable, equitable, and democratic local economy.

Business Solutions

Business Development

Sales: Our approach is to focus on a particular market or a particular (set of) client(s), often for a targeted revenue number.
Marketing: Our team designs and creates marketing strategies involving promotion and advertising aimed towards the successful sale of products to end-customers.
Management/Business Planning: The business development team will determine the scope of projects and planning based on cost- and time-related assessments. The project management/implementation team will then swing into action to work towards the desired goal.
Product Development: Our team will help develop an idea from concept to production. Product Management: FBS will manage a product from end-to-end, starting with the process of developing and marketing the product.

Business Optimization

Cloud Services: In a rapidly changing technology environment, the need to transform evolving processes and systems to meet emerging business requirements is of paramount importance. This digital transformation demands a specific expertise and a set of practices to align business focus with technology solutions. Cloud Solution architecture top the list of most important practices executed before any tech solution development begins. As a service, we can provide your business with excellent solutions such as: Designing, describing, and managing the solution engineering in relation to specific business problems. Leading the practice and introducing the overall technical vision for a particular solution. Managed in-house, solution architecture consulting as a specific set of services. Developing a cloud strategy and coordinating the adaptation process Establish best practices for cloud across the company Selecting cloud providers and vetting third-party services Oversee governance and mitigate risk Work closely with IT security to monitor privacy and develop incident-response procedures Managing budgets and estimating cost
Processes Optimization: Our team will optimize a business process by setting a parameter without violating some constraint. The purpose is to minimizing cost and maximizing throughput and/or efficiency.
Processes & procedures: FBS will create a procedure that define how to implement one or several activities of a business process. We identify the sequence of steps, and specifies for each step - what needs to be done, when, and by whom.
Automation: our team will assess and analyze specific process and procedures in order to determine if digital transformation is applicable. We help streamline a business for simplicity, achieve digital transformation, increase service quality, improve service delivery or contain costs.

Business Relationship Management

Customer Relationships: The team will ensure that the client(s) continues to understand the needs of existing customers and establishes relationships with potential new customers.
Identify Service Requirements: The team will ensure to understand and document the desired outcome of a service, and to decide if the customer's need(s) can be fulfilled using an existing service offering or if a new or changed service must be created. Sign up Customers to Standard Services: The team will to capture customer requirements and agree service level targets with customers who request the provision of existing standard services
Satisfaction Survey: We will plan, carry out and evaluate regular customer satisfaction surveys. We aim in this process to learn about areas where customer expectations are not being met before customers are lost to alternative service providers.
Handle Customer Complaints: We will record customer complaints and compliments, to assess the complaints and to instigate corrective action if required.
Monitor Customer Complaints: We will continuously monitor the processing status of outstanding customer complaints and to take corrective action if required.
